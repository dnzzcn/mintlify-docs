## More Than a Chatbot

Maia is a **general-purpose AI assistant** designed to work alongside you as an autonomous teammate. Unlike chatbots that simply answer questions, Maia plans and executes complex workflows to get real work done.

## Massively Multi-Agent Chain-of-Thought

At its core, Maia uses a revolutionary approach called **massively multi-agent chain-of-thought**:

1. **Thinks**: Analyzes your request and understands the goal
2. **Plans**: Breaks down complex tasks into semantic steps
3. **Delegates**: Assigns each sub-task to specialized agents
4. **Executes**: Coordinates multiple agents working in parallel

This means Maia can handle work that would normally require a team of specialists, from research and data analysis to document creation and web automation.
