## What is Maia?

Maia is a **general-purpose AI assistant** that orchestrates multiple state-of-the-art AI agents to deliver the best results for every task. Unlike traditional chatbots, Maia plans and executes complex workflows autonomously, making it a true AI teammate for your business.

<Note>Looking for your workspace? Visit [maia.is](https://maia.is)!</Note>
